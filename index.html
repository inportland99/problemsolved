I'm on a mission to make math irresistible.

Every day computation skills become less valuable and thinking become more valuable. School math over values memorization and under emphasizes thinking skills.

I have always had an affinity for math. Powered by my love of math, I earned a Ph.D. in Physics in 1999 which led to a decade in R&D at Intel. In 2008, I quit my job and founded Math Plus Academy, an after-school STEM enrichment program. I'm also proud to be a founding member of The Global Math Project.
