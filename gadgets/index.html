Math Gadgets

Interactive tools for mathematical exploration and discovery!

Multiplication Connect Four

A two-player strategy game combining multiplication facts with connect four. Choose factors to claim products on the board and be the first to get four in a row!

Eight Queens

Can you place 8 queens on a chess board so none of them attack each other? Start with smaller boards and work your way up to the classic 8×8 challenge.

Ruler Investigation

A mathematical puzzle about optimal measurement. Place the fewest marks on a ruler to measure every integer length. A great introduction to combinatorics!
