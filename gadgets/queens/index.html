Eight Queens

Can you place all 8 queens on the board without having them attack each other?

How to Play

  1. Click anywhere on the chess board to place a queen.
  2. Use the Check My Work button to see if any queens are attacking one another. Problems are highlighted in red.
  3. Click on any queen to remove it from the board.

If you find the 8×8 challenge difficult, try a smaller board first. The goal is to place as many queens as there are rows.

Helper Mode

For smaller boards, you can turn on helper mode. When activated, all attacked cells turn red when you place a queen, making it easier to find safe spots.
