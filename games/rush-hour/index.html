How to Play

Goal: Slide the red car to the right exit on row 3.

  1. Click any car to select it (it glows).
  2. Click the arrows on each car, or use the keyboard arrows, to slide it.
  3. Cars can only move along their own axis — horizontal cars slide left/right; vertical cars slide up/down.
  4. Move blocking cars out of the way until the red car can escape!

Press Esc to deselect a car.
