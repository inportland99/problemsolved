Brilliant Cards

A custom deck of cards for use in the math classroom

The cards are a reminder to focus on the infinite ways students can display their gifts in the classroom that go beyond academic knowledge.

One side has words to inspire you to see various forms of brilliance. The other side allows you to write a personalized note to the student.

If you love these cards and would like some for your classroom, let me know. If they become popular, I'll start producing them in larger quantities so you can order them from this site.
